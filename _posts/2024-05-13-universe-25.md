American [Ethologist](https://www.google.com/url?sa=t&source=web&rct=j&opi=89978449&url=https://dictionary.cambridge.org/dictionary/english/ethologist&ved=2ahUKEwiNsOfrvIqGAxU_d_UHHYuXAT8QFnoECCMQAw&usg=AOvVaw2qsEdtLiWY_HqTQVz6gNgK) conducted this experiment to test the effects of utopian condition in rodents.

## Experiment Setup

John designed an utopian environment "Universe 25" providing ample food, water and nesting material. Aiming to study the behavior of rodents on these conditions.

The setup was 9-square foot enclosure, divided into four interconnected pens. Each pen had water, food and nesting material. It could easily accomodate 5000 rodent.
