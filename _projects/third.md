## Motivation

Human's nowadays easily tend to get depressed, think too much about someone's saying, lot of mental health issues are popping up.
This is a small step towards using the AI to democratize and help individuals who are too afraid to talk to doctor/stranger/therapist about thier issue, use AI at thier hand to help themselves.

## Features

1 . It is the first iteration, so basically it only has a text-to-text communication channel.

2. It is heavily guardrailed to never suggest a medication/drug to any user.

3. If it senses the condition is too worse to handle if provide ways to cope up, and then speak with a proper medical professional.
